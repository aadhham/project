Arms and elbows

Cow face pose:

Steps:

1) From a seated position raise your left elbow up to the side of your head.

2) your hand facing below your spine.

3) Use your other hand to pull your left elbow over to the right while your hand moves

4) below your spine.

5) If it’s comfortable you can flex your right arm and raise your right hand up to pull your left hand.

6) Hold the position for up to 1 minute.

7) Repeat the exercise on the opposite side.

Standing arm lifts

Steps:

1) Make fists with your hands and make them in front of your hips.

2) Inhale as you raise your arms above your head so that your hands meet above your head.

3) Lower back to the original position.

4) Repeat it 10 times.

Fingers up and down stretch:

This exercise is to extend the forearms, hands, and wrists, nothing hits a simple fingers up and fingers down, it’s done to loosen up everything and maintain range of movement. This means it can be useful if you sit and write all day long.

Muscles worked: extensor carpi radialis longus, extensor carpi radialis brevis, extensor digitorum, brachialis, brachioradialis, flexor carpi.

Steps:

1) Extend your right arm out in front of you, bring it at shoulder height. Pull your fingers up, as if you’re going to put your palm against a wall.

2) With your left hand, gently down back on the fingers of your right hand. Breathe here and maintain this stretch for at least 3 deep breaths.

3) Let your right wrist limp, fingers pointing down. Now gently pull the back of your hand.

4) Keep your fingers relaxed while breathing in at least 3 times .

5) Repeat on the other side.
